What This is All About

 This program consists of four business clubs: Economics Club, Investment Club, Entrepreneurship Club and Launch Club.
 We aim to create a cohesive, comprehensive and entertaining business extracurricular program for all SMUS students who are interested in different areas of business. We hope to inspire future business leaders who are ethical and innovative.
 If you sign up for at least three clubs mentioned above, you are automatically enrolled. If you meet the attendance requirement for three of them, you will get an extra ROA for SMUS Business leaders. By the end of the year, our leadership team would select and award top 10 members who demonstrated outstanding leadership, insights and passion for business.
